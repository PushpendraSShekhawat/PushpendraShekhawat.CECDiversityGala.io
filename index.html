    About us

    Diversity Gala

    The Diversity Gala is a professional networking conference hosted by the University of South Carolina's diverse student organizations to promote diversity in STEM and celebrate their contributions to the College of Engineering and Computing while providing students, alumni, faculty, and industry leaders with the opportunity for professional development. ​

    Testimonials

    I had the honor of being on the executive board of the Inaugural 2022 CEC Diversity Gala at UofSC. As a Latina in STEM, knowing that I was a part of the group that gave our diverse students within the college of engineering and computing,  a professional event focused on diversity in STEM, was incredibly rewarding. After four months of planning, it was fulfilling seeing it all come together, where students and professionals were able to enjoy a free dinner and expand their network. I would like to extend my gratitude not only to my executive team, but also to all the professionals and students that dedicated their time and knowledge to make this event possible." 

    Karolina De La Paz 

    "As a first generation and minority student, I appreciate the CEC faculty and students for providing a networking event that celebrates the diversity at the College of Engineering and Computing. During my experience, I was able to enjoy a delicious meal while learning about different engineering fields and backgrounds. I would love to see the gala become a tradition for minority students to feel seen and encouraged for many years to come."

    Denise Garcia Mesa

    "I had the pleasure of attending the Diversity Gala and it was truly a great experience! Not only did I have the opportunity to meet and network with a diverse group of individuals, but I also had the chance to meet my future coworker for my summer internship. The event provided a fantastic opportunity to expand my network, and I highly recommend it to anyone who wants to broaden their horizons.

     

    Aside from the professional benefits, I also had a lot of fun and met many new students who I now consider friends. The event was well-organized, the atmosphere was welcoming, and the energy was infectious. I felt like I was part of a community that celebrated diversity and inclusion, which was truly inspiring.

    ​

    Overall, I can't recommend the Diversity Gala enough. It was an unforgettable experience that I will always cherish. If you're looking for a chance to meet new people, expand your network, and have some fun while doing it, then this is the event for you!"

    Jacob Vaught
